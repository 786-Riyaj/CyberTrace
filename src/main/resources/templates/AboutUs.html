About CyberTrace

CyberTrace is a comprehensive cyber crime reporting and digital investigation platform developed to tackle the growing threats of online scams, financial frauds, and organized cyber crimes.

Digital Investigation Hub

The platform offers a secure and centralized environment where individuals and organizations can report cyber incidents and submit critical digital evidence such as transaction records, screenshots, and logs. By streamlining the investigation process, CyberTrace significantly reduces manual workload and improves operational efficiency for cyber cells.

Real-time status updates & monitoring
Structured communication lifecycle

Security & Transparency

Acting as a strong bridge between victims and law enforcement, we ensure transparency and accountability. Advanced access controls, secure data storage, and audit logging ensure that sensitive information remains protected at every stage.

Designed with scalability in mind, CyberTrace maintains performance and reliability across large-scale deployments, ensuring justice is never delayed by technology limits.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect organizations from evolving cyber threats by leveraging deep digital forensics and AI-driven intelligence. We work to identify, analyze, and neutralize cyber crimes efficiently, ensuring that sensitive data and digital assets remain secure at all times

Our Vision

CyberTrace envisions a fraud-free digital world where cyber security is proactive rather than reactive. We aim to become a global leader by building intelligent technologies capable of detecting and preventing malicious activities before they cause harm.
